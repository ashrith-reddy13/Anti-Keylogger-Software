Information Security

Welcome! Here are some tips to help you stay secure online and prevent keyloggers:

  • Use strong passwords: Create passwords that are hard to guess and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Enable two-factor authentication: Add an extra layer of security to your accounts.
  • Avoid suspicious links: Be cautious about clicking links in emails or messages from unknown sources.
  • Keep software updated: Regularly update your operating system and applications to patch vulnerabilities.
  • Use anti-malware tools: Install reliable antivirus software to detect and remove malicious programs.

By following these tips, you can reduce your risk of being targeted by keyloggers or other cyber threats.
